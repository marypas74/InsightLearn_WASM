Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about InsightLearn courses, pricing, certificates, and more

Getting Started

What is InsightLearn? +

InsightLearn is an AI-powered online learning platform offering 500+ expert-led courses in programming, web development, data science, cloud computing, business, and design. Founded in 2024, we serve over 50,000 students worldwide with personalized learning experiences powered by our AI assistant.

How do I create an account? +

Creating an account is free and takes just a minute. Click the "Sign Up" button, enter your email and create a password, or sign up instantly with Google. Once registered, you can browse courses, save favorites, and start learning immediately.

Is there a free trial available? +

Many courses offer free preview lessons so you can evaluate the content before purchasing. Additionally, we have a selection of completely free courses for beginners to get started with programming and technology fundamentals.

Courses & Learning

What programming languages and technologies do you cover? +

We offer courses in Python, JavaScript, TypeScript, React, Angular, Vue.js, Node.js, Java, C#, .NET, Go, Rust, SQL, MongoDB, AWS, Azure, Google Cloud, Docker, Kubernetes, Machine Learning, Data Science, and many more technologies.

Do courses include hands-on projects? +

Yes! Most courses include practical projects and coding exercises. You'll build real-world applications, complete assignments, and create portfolio pieces that demonstrate your skills to potential employers.

Can I access courses on mobile devices? +

Yes! InsightLearn is fully responsive and works on smartphones, tablets, and desktops. You can watch video lessons, take notes, complete quizzes, and interact with the AI assistant on any device with a web browser.

What is the AI Learning Assistant? +

Our AI Learning Assistant is a 24/7 chatbot powered by advanced language models that helps you with course questions, explains concepts, provides code examples, and offers personalized learning recommendations. It's available in every course and can understand context from your current lesson.

Pricing & Payments

How much do courses cost? +

Course prices range from free to EUR 99.99. Most premium courses are priced between EUR 29.99 and EUR 49.99. We frequently offer discounts and promotions, with some courses available for free during special events.

What payment methods do you accept? +

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), PayPal, and various local payment methods depending on your region. All payments are processed securely with 256-bit SSL encryption.

What is your refund policy? +

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on all course purchases. If you're not satisfied with a course, contact our support team within 30 days of purchase for a full refund, no questions asked.

How long do I have access to a course? +

Once you purchase a course, you have lifetime access to all course materials, including any future updates the instructor makes. You can learn at your own pace and revisit content whenever you need a refresher.

Certificates & Credentials

Do I get a certificate after completing a course? +

Yes! Upon completing any course, you receive a verified Certificate of Completion that you can share on LinkedIn, add to your resume, or download as a PDF. Certificates include your name, course title, completion date, and a unique verification code.

Are InsightLearn certificates recognized by employers? +

Our certificates demonstrate your commitment to learning and skill development. While they are not accredited degrees, many employers value continuous learning and practical skills gained through online courses, especially when combined with portfolio projects.

Instructors

Can I become an instructor on InsightLearn? +

Yes! We welcome expert instructors to share their knowledge. Instructors earn 80% of course revenue and get access to our course creation tools, analytics dashboard, and marketing support. Visit our 'Become an Instructor' page to apply.

Support

How do I contact support? +

You can reach our support team via email at support@insightlearn.cloud, through the contact form on our website, or using the in-app chat feature. We typically respond within 24 hours during business days.

Still have questions?

Our support team is here to help you with any questions not covered in our FAQ.
