Frequently Asked Questions

How can I contact InsightLearn support?

You can reach us by email at support@insightlearn.cloud. Fill out the contact form on our website for faster routing to the right team. We respond within 24 hours on business days.

What is InsightLearn's support response time?

Our support team responds within 24 hours on business days (Monday-Friday, 9:00-18:00 CET). Premium subscribers receive priority support with faster response times.

How do I report a technical issue?

Email support@insightlearn.cloud with a description of the issue, the course name, your browser type, and any error messages. Screenshots are helpful for faster resolution.
