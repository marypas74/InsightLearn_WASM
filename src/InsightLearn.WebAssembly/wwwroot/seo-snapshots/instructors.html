How to Get Started

Step 1: Apply Online

Fill out the instructor application form with your expertise, teaching experience, and proposed course topics. No prior online teaching experience is required.

Step 2: Get Approved

Our team reviews your application within 48 hours. We look for subject matter expertise and a passion for teaching.

Step 3: Create Your Course

Use our professional tools to build your course. Upload videos, create quizzes, add resources, and let our AI generate subtitles automatically.

Step 4: Publish & Earn

Once your course is reviewed and approved, it goes live on the platform. Start earning from day one with up to 80% revenue share.
