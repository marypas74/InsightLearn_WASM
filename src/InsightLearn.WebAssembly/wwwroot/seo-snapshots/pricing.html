Frequently Asked Questions

Are there free courses on InsightLearn?

Yes! InsightLearn offers a selection of high-quality free courses. You can start learning without a credit card. Free courses include community support and basic learning tools.

How much do premium courses cost?

Premium courses range from €9.99 to €199.99 depending on the content depth and duration. Each purchase includes lifetime access and a certificate of completion.

What is included in the subscription plan?

The subscription gives you unlimited access to all 500+ courses, AI-powered learning tools, priority support, progress tracking, and certificates. Monthly is €19.99/mo, annual is €149.99/yr (save 37%).

Do you offer refunds?

Yes, we offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on all purchases. If you're not satisfied, contact support@insightlearn.cloud for a full refund.
